Must Have Lingerie Types For Your Honeymoon

October 18, 2017 1:45 pm

Weddings can be agonizing and stressful, but the thought of unwinding at your honeymoon helps you get through it all. Lingerie being the most vital part of your honeymoon, helps couples make their night special in style. So here’s some must have lingerie types for your honeymoon;

 

The Angelic "Sexy Jeanie"

LKR 2700/-

 

For when you’re playing to be a sexy angel. Or just plain sexy. This transparent lace material with a deep neckline is available at Midnight Divas in UK size 8-14. Available in colours; Black and White, for your monochrome moods.

 
 

The Elegant "Satin and Lace Robe"

LKR 3550/-

 

A comfortable yet sensual look to be lounging around in. Or if you’re just looking for something light and airy on  your honeymoon to put on after a shower to keep things interesting. This soft satin material with lace edges is available at Amante in size S-XL. Availability of Colours; Red, Black.

 

The Seductive "Anna Malle"  

LKR 3200/-

 

 

This is an off-shoulder baby doll with lace trimmings and frilly sleeves, this hot number is designed by Midnight Divas especially for brides. A sure purchase if you’re looking for an extra special night, if you know what I mean. This free size item is available in black and white colours.

 

Ever Fancy "Fish-net Pantyhose" 

LKR 795-899/-

 

 

 

Wear this discreetly under a dress, as an exciting surprise for the husband during a night out. It’s available in black and red, so you could purchase it depending on your sexy outfit. Find this item at Secrets in free sizes making it a fast moving item among many couples. Go ahead, surprice your man this night. 

 

The Delicate feel of "Vova Anna Night Set"

LKR 3995/-

A naughty, hot night set for a sophisticated alluring look. The texture is a delicate and soft viscose fabric with a touch of lace. The wired cups made of enticing lace while the body makes you feel soft as a flower. This night set is a pair of top and shorts with adjustable straps. Available in sizes S, M and L available at pinkbutterfly.lk

 

The Charming "Festeve Chemise" 

LKR 3595/-

 
 

A simple, seductive look for the night, if your low key and trying to get under his skin. It is styled with embroided cups and lightweight micro fiber. The chemise is available in XL at Amante. Dazzle your spounce with this engimatic redness that will be hard to keep his hands away from you.  

 

The Enchanting "Satin and Lace Chemise"   

LKR 2950/-

This chemise could be another low key look, unless paired with a some enticing killer heels. You're sure to catch him off guard without a doubt. Comfortable silk chemise with lace borders that are available in the same colours as the robe (Red, Black) therefore you can mix and match, however you wish. Available in size S-XL at Amante.

 

Miss Fiery "Baby Doll with G-string" 

LKR 2000-3000/-

 

This baby doll screams sexy. The lacy texture and side slit gives the viewer the oomph it needs! The nice elegant touch, and the sensual color will do the needful in making you the siren on the night. This item of oozing passion is available at Gali’s Lingerie and can be found in free size to suit the goddesses in all shapes.

 

Hot & Bold "Vera Body Stocking" 

LKR 2700/-

Intricate and arousing lace detailed crotchless body stocking is for those bold and daring Goddesses aiming for a steamy night or if you’re just looking to keep him indoors all day long. Pair this with thigh high stockings for a sensual and kinkier look, like shown above. Available at midnight divas from UK size 8-14.

 

The Irresistable Gorsenia Felicita Chemise

LKR 2895/-

An erotic piece despite being graceful, gives you the irresistible touch you’re looking for. It is made of shiny silk satin that is smooth on the skin, while the cups are enhanced with lace fabric. A Perfect feminine touch to your night. Available in size S, M, XL at pinkbutterfly.lk

 

Be sure to carry a couple of these lingeries to your honeymoon. Each of them help you achieve different looks and therefore keep things exciting during your travel and your time together. So let’s get ahead of things and make that husband happy. Happy Honeymoon!
