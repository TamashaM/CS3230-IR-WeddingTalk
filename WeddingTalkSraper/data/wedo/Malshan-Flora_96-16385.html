About Malshan Flora   

Malshan Flora consists of approximately fifty personnel of florists, designers, sales staff and general helpers who stick together to make you proud at the most beautiful moment in your life. Their expertise in artificial flower creations and flower decorations is one of the best in the field. Whether it is a complex, traditional or western arrangement, they assure you the right finish for your utmost satisfaction.
