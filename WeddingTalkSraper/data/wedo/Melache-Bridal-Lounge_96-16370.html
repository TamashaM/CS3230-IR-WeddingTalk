About Melache Bridal Lounge

Melache Bridal Lounge is the designer boutique to bring alive your ultimate wedding dress. The team at Melache believes in identifying "you" and working to design and create the best and the most beautiful dress that will make an unforgettable memory.

Your dress ought to show you in your purest form and portray your femininity in the most sophisticated way. Be your style classic, romantic, modern, or contemporary their highly talented designers will work with you closely to bring it out in the best form suited for you to make a unique statement on your most precious day. 

Specialise in

Bridals and evening dresses

Gown / Suit Collections

Bridals are catered to western and modern eastern designs with a very contemporary twist to their styles.

Wedding Categories

Bridal and entire group attire. Additionally, they do the groom and groomsmen attire too.
