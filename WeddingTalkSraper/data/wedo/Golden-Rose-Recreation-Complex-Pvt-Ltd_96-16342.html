Golden Rose Recreation Complex (Pvt) Ltd

November 14, 2017 3:05 pm

 

About Golden Rose Recreation Complex (Pvt) Ltd

Golden Rose is located on the outskirts of Colombo City, it is an ideal location for an array of functions, from large weddings to conferences and even cocktail parties. The grandiose, modern building offers the full range of facilities and amenities required to host any social or corporate function.

Extra Perks

The upscale extras include;

  • Dressing rooms for bridal couples
  • Spacious studio areas for lively photographs
  • Sumptuous meals prepared by finest chefs catering to an extremely well-heeled VIP clientele that often includes the elite.

 

Other facilities

  • Richly Textured Elegant Curtains
  • Ample Parking
  • LED Lights and wall washes to enhance the ambience
  • Table floral arrangements and hall setups and more depending on your needs supported by an array of catering options to choose from.
