Must Have Wedding Photos Of Your Big Day!

October 25, 2017 9:56 am

It takes almost a life time to prepare for your big day; well at least it feels that way. There’s so much effort and hard work brought in to make your dream wedding come to life. Each excited and anxious couple wants a picture perfect day filled with love and happiness. Every couple wishes for all the wonderful memories made on their big day to be captured through the lens of a photographer which will then be developed into images that will last a lifetime. 

The bride and groom will share thousands of precious moments together with their loved ones on the day of the wedding, which is why it’s important to keep your preferred photographer informed on what photographs are a must.

As the bride and groom, it is important for the both of you to be relaxed and stress-free on the day of the wedding. Therefore it is advised that you discuss all your requirements with the photographer prior to the day of the wedding. 

While you’re beautiful day unfolds, we understand that it will not be easy to capture each and every moment, but do make sure you capture all the moments listed below.

 

The Ring Shot

Image credit : fotocube.com 

“From this day on, now and forever more”, the wedding rings symbolise your vows and commitment to each other. It’s said that the ring finger connects directly to your heart. A beautiful photograph of the rings is a definite must-have. 

 

The Bride and Mother

Image source : thecrimsonbride.com

Every mother longs to see her beautiful girl as a bride, she waits to see her daughter as a dazzling princess on this special day.  It’s the day her little girl leaves the humble nest to build one of her own. Capture all these emotions of you and your mother.

 

The Father of the Bride 

image source : pinterest.com

Giving his little girl away is not easy, from this day on, his precious girl will be under the protection of the man she has chosen to marry. A father goes through multiple happy and sad moments as he hands her over. Capture your father’s emotions with a very special photograph together.

 

The bride and groom hand in hand  

Image courtsey : Cloud Attic Photography

The happiest day of your lives is finally here; you are now husband and wife and are ready to begin your journey together to make a life full of happiness and love. The love you feel for each other on this special day is hard to be put into words, so capture it with an amazing photograph.

 

All That's perfect  

Image source : thebigfatindianwedding.com

As the bride to be, you probably spent months going through hundreds of images in search of that perfect dress, perfect shoes - basically the perfect everything. Snap a photograph of all that’s perfect on your big day. Admire your good taste and take a deep comforting breath of relief to assure yourself that, you got it right.

 

Your best-maids and you

Image source : Studio Anantra 

Your best maids will be just as happy as you on your wedding day; they know all your little secrets and all your dreams. Your maids are most often your best and most trusted friends, click the happiest moments together with the bunch; after all, you won’t be a bachelorette henceforth. 

 

YOU – All dolled up 


Image courtsey : Dammika De Alwis Studio 

As the bride, you probably knew how you wanted to look on your wedding day since you turned sixteen. You would have imagined yourself looking breathtakingly beautiful, you wait to see the happiness in the eyes of your husband-to-be as he sees you for the first time. This is the day that all eyes will be on you. Capture your beauty in a stunning portrait picture of you on the day of your wedding.

We have all scrolled through and celebrated our parents’ wedding pictures and so have many generations before us. Let’s make the most celebrated day of your life last forever by capturing all the beauty, joy, love and happiness through a set of stunning images.
