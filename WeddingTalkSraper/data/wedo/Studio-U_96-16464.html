About Studio U  

 

Studio U have made an impressive name for themselves in the wedding industry and are now a household name here in Sri Lanka for professional photography and videography. Introducing the storybook concept in wedding albums, they are one of the pioneering teams headed by Mr. Kushantha Hewapathirana to use drone cameras for wedding shoots resulting in them being appointed brand ambassador of the Sony Alpha Series cameras. 

Studio U strives on using this cutting-edge technology to create and innovate new photographic concepts that keep them ahead of the market, unlike others. Mr. Hewapathirana conducts workshops and seminars to share his knowledge and experience in photography with potential and amateur photographers, aspiring to make a name in this highly competitive field.
