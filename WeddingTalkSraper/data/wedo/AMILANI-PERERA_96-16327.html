About Amilani Perera designs

The exotic luxury fashion brand, ‘AMILANI PERERA’ is a ready to wear brand which incorporates unique chic styling together with the traditional Sri Lankan cultural motifs. The brand is well-known for its unique bespoke designer wear in Sri Lanka.

Specialise in

Designer Bridal wear and Bridal retinue

Gown / Suit Collections

Weddings by Amilani

Wedding Categories

Designer bridal sarees, traditional kandian bridal, bridesmaid dresses, mother of the bride dresses, wedding dresses, custom designs

Awards

  • Amilani Perera is also the official designer for the Airport and Aviation Services of Sri Lanka (AASL).
  • Emerged as a finalist in the Young Creative Entrepreneur Design and Fashion Award in 2012 organised by the British Council.
  • Emerged as a finalist in the Sri Lanka Fashion and Apparel Awards 2013 under the "Industry Young Blood" and "Sustainable Fashion" award categories.
  • Emerged as one of the top ten finalists at the ‘denim deconstruction’ competition in Singapore organised by the GAS Jeans & Catalog Magazine
