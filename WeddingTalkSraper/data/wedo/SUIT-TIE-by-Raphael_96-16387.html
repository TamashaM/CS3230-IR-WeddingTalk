About SUIT & TIE by Raphael

Suit & Tie by Raphael is a top ranked tailored suit boutique in Colombo that provides Bespoke & Made to Measure suits that fit you perfectly. Full canvas, half canvas and fused suits are mastered by their professional tailors. They offer the trendiest Italian cuts and silhouettes in town. A wide range of the finest fabrics which are sourced from Italy, England, Bangkok and Hong Kong are available at their own design studio. They also offer the world’s best luxury fabric brands such as Ermenegildo Zegna, Scabal, Reda, Donetella & Caccioppoli Napoli. Their suits are fully customised according to the customer requirement. A wide range of linings and a host of custom details such as the fit, lapel shape, vents, pockets style and buttons can be chosen by their customers. Their professional designers and expert tailors will work with you to craft a suit so superb, it will last for years to come. A sound investment for your professional life, wedding or formal occasion.

Your dress ought to show you in your purest form and portray your femininity in the most sophisticated way. Be your style classic, romantic, modern, or contemporary their highly talented designers will work with you closely to bring it out in the best form suited for you to make a unique statement on your most precious day.
