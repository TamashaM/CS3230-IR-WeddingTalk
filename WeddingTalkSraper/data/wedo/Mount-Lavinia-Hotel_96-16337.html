Mount Lavinia Hotel

November 13, 2017 1:12 pm

About Mount Lavinia Hotel

With a romantic history that was entwined upon the tale of Mount Lavinia, which the very foundation of the hotel was built on 211 years ago, you would be right to assume that when it comes to hosting the most romantic day in your life, they go the extra mile. 

 

Extra Perks

Apart from the standard amenities, Mount Lavinia Hotel offers varieties of the below, 

  • Seat covers free of charge 
  • Welcome drink free of charge*
  • Corkage-free of charge 

(* - Terms & Conditions apply)

Reviews

 

 - Reviewed on 31.07.2017 by Thavisha Prabhani

We had our wedding in the Grand Ballroom at Mt Lavinia hotel last month. We are so so happy about the service provided by Mt Lavinia. Miss Nimanthi (Manager) was invaluable from the day we booked our wedding at Mt Lavinia. She helped us with everything from table arrangements, guest lists, picking the menu to book rooms for our guests. Mt Lavinia was able to cater to our needs just as we wanted, despite the many last minute changes. On the day, everything went perfectly. The staff knew exactly what needed to be done and made sure our guests were well looked after. The food was amazing - we received lots of great comments from our guests. The hotel gave us changing rooms, extra lighting, early breakfast and anything else we asked for. All in all, we are so happy and grateful for the amazing service provided to us by Mt Lavinia on the most important day of our life.
