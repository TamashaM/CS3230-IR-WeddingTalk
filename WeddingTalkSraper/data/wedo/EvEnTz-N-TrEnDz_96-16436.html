About EvEnTz N TrEnDz  

Eventz N Trendz ensures that every aspect of your wedding flows seamlessly and according to your wishes and specifications. From finding vendors, decorating your wedding ceremony & reception, to allowing you time to mingle with your guests, they conscientiously and unobtrusively orchestrate the logistics of your special day. 

They have planned the nuptials of celebrities like Santhush Weeraman, Rangana Herath, Farveez Maharoof, Ajantha Mendis, Lahiru Perera, Nadini Premadasa, Narmadha Abeywardena, Saheli Gamage etc. They were also interviewed by BBC News during the period of the Royal Wedding of Kate & William when BBC News covered traditional weddings around the world.

 Meet the owner

Uzmaan and Iyanthie have come a long way since they started at Ceylon Continental Hotel as an amateur event and wedding planners. Moving on from there to the Cinnamon Grand, which matured their knowledge further, not only on Events but also on Food & Beverage, Quality and Service Standards, they are now known for their commitment and enthusiasm to make whatever event that comes their way a success, through their company “Eventz N Trendz” which was founded in 2009.
