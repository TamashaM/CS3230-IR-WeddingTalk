 - Reviewed on 07.07.2014 Reviewed by Michael David

I recently held my wedding at Bolgoda 360 and was quite impressed with the level of service I received, The management and staff were quite helpful and accommodated our requests and changes without any fuss, the food tasted awesome and I continue to hear good things from my guests about the food and location. I would also like to thank Bolgoda 360 for giving me ample time to set up our custom deco'r which was a cause of major concern for me. yet the management ensured the calendar was clear of any other function for which I am very grateful. Although my wedding was held in the garden I quite liked the layout of the hall as well as they have kept it simple and elegant. The location which is not far from Galle Road combined with the spacious halls and beautiful garden makes it an ideal location for any function be it a wedding, party or corporate event.

Thank you Bolgoda 360 and all the best for your future! Cheers, Michael
