Top 05 Magical Honeymoons you can budget

February 01, 2018 10:15 am

Ahhhh... The honeymoon! It’s probably the most anticipated yet magical vacation of a whole lifetime.

It takes a lot of effort, hard work and patience to organize and execute that perfect wedding and once it’s all done a relaxing break is what every couple looks forward to.

A honeymoon is one of those once in a lifetime getaways, where the newly married lovebirds make plenty of memories, therefore it is important to make sure that your first getaway as “Mr & Mrs” is nothing but magical.

Let us take you through our top 05 budget, yet captivating honeymoon destinations right here in our beautiful mother Sri Lanka.
 

#05 Anantaya Resort & Spa Chilaw

Photo: Tripadvisor.com 

Ananataya Resort & Spa Chilaw, is a spacious, beautifully located hideout with a breathtaking view. The resort is tastefully decorated with scrumptious cuisines and al-a-carte services.

Out of all its luxuriously decorated rooms, we pick the “Suite” as the most suitable for a honeymoon couple. Each suite is tucked away on the second floor with private balconies on both sides, each offering a spectacular view of the lagoon and sea.

Each suite is 1,499 sq ft and has its own Jacuzzi, mini bar, living and dining area along with all other modern amenities.

The resort is offering a massive 30% discount to all honeymooners until the 31st of October 2018. 

#04 Avani Resort Kalutara

Photo: Avani Resort Kalutara 

This resort is an islander’s paradise, if you two are sun and sand lovers this is the ideal honeymoon destination for you. Not too far away from Colombo the drive is quite exciting.

The resort is out of a catalogue with luxuriously decorated rooms and mouth-watering cuisines. We pick the 60 SQM Junior suites as the most spacious and comfortable for the happy honeymooners.

The suite is decorated to ease the stress away with its own bathtub and rain shower, tasteful furniture and a fresh fruit platter upon arrival. Equipped with all modern amenities, a private bar and balcony offering a breathtaking view, this is truly paradise.
 

#03 Uga Ulagalla

Image source: booking.com

A nature lover’s dreamland, this rest is breathtakingly beautiful, soothingly calm and is hidden away from all the hassle and bustle of city life, call it a honeymooner’s paradise.

We pick the “Ulagalla Chalets” as the perfect choice to spend your getaway. Each chalet is 850 sq ft and offers a view of a pictures garden or beautiful paddy field; equipped with all modern amenities it has its own private plunge pool, 24 hour room service, its very own sundeck and gloriously versatile cuisine options. 

A definite must check spot for all you future honeymooners. 

#02 The Paradise Resort & Spa Dambulla

Image source: booking.com

Located in the historical city of Dambulla, There’s always plenty to see, historical monuments, great lakes, lushes greenery and plenty of wildlife. The resort takes you back in time, lets you breathe in the fresh air and relax like never before.

The suites are amazing, each suite is a massive 130 square meters and offers all modern amenities each suite is equipped with its own Jacuzzi, living and dining area, this place is so tranquil that you will never want your honeymoon to end.

They offer a variety of Sri- Lankan and international cuisines and 24-hour room service. If you love nature, history and tranquillity, this will truly be your paradise. 
 

#01 Habarana Village by Cinnamon

Photo: Cinnamon Hotels 

Our number 01 budget honeymoon destination goes to Habarna Lodge by Cinnamon.

Let us tell you why.

We pick “The Kingfisher Lodge/Suite”; the lodge is private and spacious, 875 sq ft to be precise. Each suite offers a breathtaking view of the lake, with outdoor furniture that lets you enjoy the tranquillity and beauty of nature.

Each lodge has an open-air bathroom with an indoor bath making your showers all the more exciting. Each suite is equipped with all modern facilities which include a mini bar, spacious living area and private veranda.

One simply cannot ask for a better stay.

The food is scrumptious and the service is top notch. Your honeymoon will truly be amazing at this wonderfully breathtaking hideout.

Cinnamon Hotels and Resorts are offering a 10% discount to all you lovely couples out there if you book through www.champagne.lk until September 2018. 

Memories of these few days you make will last you a lifetime, choose well and wise and enjoy the best days of your life!
