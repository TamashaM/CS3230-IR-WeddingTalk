UNIQUE WAYS TO DISPLAY YOUR WEDDING FOOD

May 16, 2018 4:59 pm

There is always one thing guests are going to remember about a wedding and that’s the food, you no more need tons of food to make your wedding look grand it’s all in how you choose to display your food.

Here are a few ways you can lay out your wedding food to make your wedding truly one to remember.

BUFFET TABLE OR FOOD STATION BACKDROPS 
A backdrop for your buffet table or food station will draw immediate attention of all your guest and will keep them inquisitive to see what’s on the table.

Your backdrop can go in line with your other wedding decorations while representing the food on the table. There are many different ways you can play around with your backdrop from giant balloons and flowers to paper cranes and chalkboards.

Image source: Pinterest.com


FOOD NAME CARDS 
It’s always a good idea to label all your food at the wedding and when doing this make it fun and interesting.

You can also add little sayings or tags related to you and your partner, after all, it's your big day. 

Image source: The Wedding Artisans

FOOD PRESENTATION 
When it comes to displaying the food in a stylish way it’s all in how you lay your table, by a few simple rules like arranging the food in groups, rows, and levels.

It’s also important to use interesting and unique furniture on your food table, this way the food on your table is organized and easy to access.

Image source: Pinterest.com

MENU BOARD 
It’s always good to let your guest know what’s in store for them so that they can prepare their tummies for the feast. There are many interesting ways to put out your menu from chalkboards to easy printables.

Image source: The Wedding Artisans

TABLE DECORATIONS 
It’s important that your food tables are attractive and colorful, you can add some decorations that go in line with the rest of the decorations of your wedding, this way your tables won’t be boring and just overflowing with food.

Image source: The Wedding Artisans

ICE CREAM STAND 
There is not one guest on your list that would say no to ice cream because after all, we all scream for ice cream! This is a fun and interesting way to serve dessert at your wedding and is sure to make your one remember.

     Image source: Pinterest.com

DIY STATIONS 
Do it your self-stations are a great way to get your guests involved and entertained, its sure that your guest will enjoy being a part of what’s on their table.
