About Dec-o-Cake 

Established in 2012 by Sherin Farman, after her early passion for confectionery art became her choice of career. Having kicked off solo where she mastered in creating and conceptualising unique ‘pieces of art’, she was later joined by long-standing friend Ayesha Zaneer, also an amazing baker and now the Head Baker at Dec-O-Cake.

Dec-O-Cake is a household name in Canada, Dubai and Sri Lanka, much to having created beautiful, elegant and breathtaking cakes, cookies, cupcakes and all known sweet table goodies.

At Dec-O- Cake, their goal is to transform your vision into something you can sink your teeth into.

They use fresh high-quality ingredients to ensure your cake tastes just as great as it looks!
