6 Ways to Breeze through Wedding Planning Mayhem

March 13, 2017 10:26 am

You swore you wouldn’t let the stress get to you. You assured your friends that you’d never turn into a bridezilla or groomzilla like that cousin of yours. You were pretty confident that you were on top of things. After all, this wedding was years in the making. But of course, a wedding - big or small - is almost never a walk in the park. Something is bound to go wrong. Someone is likely to screw up. Eventually, your optimistic facade fades away, and your impending wedding weighs heavily on you, leaving you overwhelmed and exhausted.
Prepare to take a breather - here are 6 ways to keep your sanity, maintain your zen and cultivate your inner peace during the wedding planning process.


Delegate tasks

It’s your wedding, but you don’t have to possessively hold onto the reigns. Find  trustworthy people you can count on to share the workload and the planning. If you have the means, pick a reliable wedding planner you are comfortable working with.


Get a massage

Treat yourself! A well deserved pampering sesh is not only a blissful experience that leaves you feeling amazing, but a seasoned masseuse will ensure that your massage is customised to suit your individual needs and help relieve muscle tension. You can choose to get a head massage or foot massage, or even just get a full body massage while you’re at it.


Deep breathing techniques / yoga

Deep breathing is actually amazing - from making you calmer to detoxifying your body to relieving pain and making you happier, the benefits make it an ideal habit for those dealing with high-stress situations. Besides, it’s almost too easy to do no matter where you are. Yoga, on the other hand, requires a little guidance for its comparatively complex postures. While yoga may not be everyone’s cup of tea - unless contorting your body into humanly impossible postures floats your boat - it improves energy and vitality, among many other things. So join a class and reap the benefits. Meanwhile, Shavasana anyone?


Stay healthy

This might sound pretty obvious - and it is! - but people underestimate how important it is to stay healthy and hydrated. This means engaging in some form of exercise - even walking, eating healthy food, and drinking plenty of water.


Take a warm/bubble bath!

For starters, just hearing of a bubble bath will likely conjure up visions of euphoria and tranquility. A warm bath, meanwhile helps relax your muscles and soothe all your aches and pains. Play some chill music, fill out that bathtub and relax your worries away!


Focus on what’s important

The perfect wedding isn’t about having the most expensive decor or the most outrageously in-demand DJ. It’s about you, your partner, and your genuine love for each other. There may be a few mishaps in your wedding plans, and things may not go according to plan, but that’s okay. Breathe, calmly assess what you can do about the situation, and move on.
