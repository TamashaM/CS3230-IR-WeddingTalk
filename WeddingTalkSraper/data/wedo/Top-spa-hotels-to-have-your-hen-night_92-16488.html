Top spa hotels to have your hen night

February 12, 2018 2:06 pm

Brides, your big day is almost upon you, but first, it’s time to kick back and have some laugh out loud fun!

Planning a wedding can be seriously stressful, so a day (or two!) of pampering in a serene environment should be a most welcome break. Just gather your closest gal pals, kick back and relax. 


Jetwing Lighthouse, Galle

Image source: jetwinghotels.com 

One of Galle’s most iconic hotels, this hotel offers tranquil and extensive Ayurveda and spa services to help you feel refreshed and rejuvenated. The hotel’s spa comes equipped with a cold plunge pool and your group will be treated to refreshing drinks and yummy snacks after you get pampered.

Rooms come with private balconies with splendid views overlooking the Indian Ocean, perfect for enjoying a few drinks with the girls. You could even go all out and book one of the three themed suites, replete with a personal butler.

Easily and quickly accessible thanks to the Southern Expressway, this hotel offers a variety of experiences and is just a short ride away from the historic Galle Fort if you and your crew want to drop by for lunch or just a yummy gelato.


Santani Resort, Kandy

Image source: santani.lk

If you’re looking for a luxury retreat in the hills to enjoy a relaxing hen-do, this is the place for you!

Located about 1.5 hours away from the hustle and bustle of Kandy city, Santani Resort offers wellness programmes in Ayurveda, detox and cleansing, as well as yoga, at its award-winning spa.

The spa includes a thermal saltwater pool, sauna and steam room treatments. The hotel will cater to any specific dietary needs of your group on request and even has a biodynamic wine and fresh juice bar.

Accommodation includes minimally furnished, spacious chalets with breath-taking views of the Hunnasgiriya and the Knuckles Mountain ranges. This hotel is all about customisation, so do feel free to contact them for bespoke packages.


Jetwing Ayurveda Pavilions, Negombo

Image source: jetwinghotels.com

Only a convenient short drive from Colombo and therefore a great option for a day trip, this is a specialised Ayurveda resort. It is the perfect tranquil escape for you and your close girlfriends to enjoy a quiet retreat without the general chaos of a hen party.

Jetwing Ayurveda Pavilions features Ayurveda therapy centers including a steam room, experienced Ayurvedic doctors and even an on-site Ayurveda pharmacy to help you have a fully immersive experience.

Your party will be able to benefit from a selection of relaxing treatments and personalised packages, perfect for soothing away that pre-wedding stress. You can even book one of the two villas, complete with private treatment areas for that extra bit of privacy as you bond with your friends over massages and other traditional therapies.


Talalla Retreat, Talalla

Image source: talallaretreat.com

If you and your party crew are the type to want some relaxation, but also enjoy some fun activities, this is the spot for you!

At the beachfront Talalla Retreat, you can combine an action-packed holiday with soothing spa treatments. Take your pick from thrilling surfing (complete with coaching and equipment provided), calming yoga, pilates or just chilling on the beautiful beach.

You can enjoy the ‘glamping’ experience in the hotel’s bamboo hut, or book the bungalow for your party to kick back and relax in private. The hotel’s spa offers a full range of treatments spanning massages, facials, body wraps and scrubs, and nail pampering.


The Fortress Resort and Spa, Koggala 

This southern coastal escape offers pure luxury and indulgence. The well-equipped spa has signature treatments as well as customised Ayurveda packages, as well as hair, skin and nail treatments and water therapies.

The Fortress has four suites on offer complete with an indoor plunge pool, personal butler service and private balconies. You can even opt for in-room dining if you want to keep the party within your room.

If your group feels like venturing out of the resort, other attractions close by including the Galle Fort, whale watching, Rumassala Hill and the Koggala Lake.


Temple tree Resort and Spa, Induruwa 

Image source: templetreeresortandspa.com

This cosy boutique hotel is just a 10 minute drive from Bentota and boasts stunning sea views. The Ayurveda spa has trained therapists and offers treatments with natural herbs at affordable prices.

You can walk down to the clean peaceful beach stretch or relax in the hotel’s pool which also features a built-in Jacuzzi. The tastefully decorated rooms and suites come with private balconies for your group to gather round to capture a breath-taking sunset, which is the perfect lighting for all those ‘groupies’.

The hotel can set up an al-fresco dining arrangement for you, so you can dine amongst the calming breeze and swaying coconut trees. If you are looking for something adventurous, head to Bentota, where you and your girls can enjoy windsurfing, water skiing, jet skiing and banana boating.
