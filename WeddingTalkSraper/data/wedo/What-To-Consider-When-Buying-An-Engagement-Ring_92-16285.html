What To Consider When Buying An Engagement Ring

February 21, 2017 12:33 pm

Rings symbolise relationships. Blokes in the hope to join the rest of who intend on impressing ‘her’ perhaps with an engagement ring, you may have to immerse yourself in some input we thought might be convenient.

Budget

Source: designexperts.co.in

Come to think of it, panic starts when grooms-to-be contemplate how much they should save from the usual earnings.  Remember, you’re not alone in this. Whether a retailer’s pledge is working to any budget for customer’s content, it is necessary that you start understanding your budget before devoting to a purchase.

Style

 

Source: shanore.com

Most Sri Lankan couples are in favour of going ring shopping together.  In that case, specialities and selections of what she really wants can be discussed in the open, all the more widening your horizons. But how far can it go? Let’s have a look:

  • Bespoke

When choosing to commission an unexampled ring piece, bear in mind, your cooperation is essential. As clients, take a rough sketch so that your local jeweller is guided towards the ring envisioned. This isn’t something to stress over if you can’t draw. They’ll work you through all the options. Wonder how long the process will take? The time to craft an individual piece may at least be a month depending on the inclusions of the tasks applicable. So best ask the jeweller to hit you up with a deadline.

Tip: Take along with you some old gold that could be recycled.

Stone Free

If your relationship doesn’t count heavily on wealth and tradition, might we suggest that you experiment with something out of the box? Either filigreed patterns might do the trick, or replacing it by using symbols instead of a status bling seems right.  Why not add a touch of your initials, perhaps a lover’s knot or include the Claddagh? What’s a Claddagh, you ask. Featuring two hands, a heart, and a crown, a Claddagh is a traditional Irish ring that demands of significant value-- including friendship, love, and loyalty, in that particular order. Seems ideal if you Lankans have no fuss choosing a European concept.

  • Vintage/Antique/Retro

The words may all strike a similar chord. Yet, how familiar are you with its nuance? We got this figured out. While “antique,” generally refers to a ring before the 50’s, “vintage” belongs to post 50’s and “retro” is applicable to what is now outdated. So, perhaps your partner comes with an incurable appetite for these.  To add, the idea of going vintage, would be a bonus if both of you plan to have a rustic wedding. However, it’s vital to be mindful of what to look for by approaching a auctioneer (well-known) for advice before embarking on this option. As with buying these valuable antiques, websites such as Ebay and Etsy offer some deals that sit on  the meritable side. It would be also smart  to watch out for our local names and numbers. Above all, kindly request for a certificate of appraisal before you venture to purchase whichever one that suits.

  • Family Heirloom

So you’re slightly tight on the budget? Remember the hungry little boy you were years ago, running to aachchi (grandma) in need of a little pocket fix? She never left you sad and teary-eyed. So, don’t you think such favours be given credit, in return atleast? Why not make the effort to check with your family/parent if any heirlooms are waiting to be passed onto the next? That way, not only will you make your grandma proud, but also your partner who is certainly going to love the family attachment you’re involving her in. After gaining the family’s permission, you could think of a more polished setting and size, given how luckily you’ll score if intended to make the leap.  

Understanding the Four C’s of a Girl’s Best Friend

 

Source: diamondsinternational.com

Cut: An “ideal diamond”  is determining its characteristic worth. A specific one has been cut to meet legitimate standards where proportions, symmetry and polish are considered. Common shapes include, the round cut, the princess, the emerald,  the heart shape, the marquise,the pear, the oval and the heart.  

Clarity: When a diamond is graded, the amount of inclusions and blemishes, invisible to the naked eye, can vastly impact its clarity and value. Diamonds containing no inclusions are not only rare but inevitably pricier.

Carat: Although this seems to be the sole factor people opt out to determine a diamond’s wealth, it should not be a decisive one. Elements may differ from one another so do check the factors along with the carat, and arrive to a conclusion that the carat weight can alone can’t determine diamond value.  

Colour: Gemological sources have vouched what strongly qualifies in relation to the colour of a diamond is ‘white’, to say this should be as colourless as possible. To identify quality, evaluations are thus developed through the involving of a categorical scale of colour grading systems. The scale is sorted alphabetically from D to Z+, with D indicating the highest, best grade possible given to diamonds that are desirable for market.

Summing Up

Engagements get you entangled to a frisson of excitement, should you know where to begin now, hoping that we’ve had you enlightened.
