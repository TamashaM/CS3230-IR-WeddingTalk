10 Wedding Destinations for Beach Lovers

February 20, 2017 2:45 pm

Sri Lanka’s beaches are globally renowned for their ever tropical resplendence. With 1600 km of turquoise blue adorning the island’s rim, it serves as an impressive backdrop for a wedding celebration. 

But to pick an ideal beach setting and bring it to spotlight can be a strenuous mission. As a result, we pulled together some of the better beach venues from across the country for a wedding reception.

 

10. Heritance Ahungalla, Ahungalla


 

Source: bookings.com

Ahungalla is a coastal town located in the Galle district of Sri Lanka. The hotel, 47 miles from Colombo, offers 3 main wedding packages for those who plan to get married - feet sunk in the butter brown sands - if the weather doesn’t prove inclement.

Venues and Packages

Of the 152 rooms and 9 suites available, the Luxury Suite consists of a private balcony, jacuzzi tub and a rain shower. There’s a committed events team to support you with all the arrangements, inclusive of the packages namely; Heritance Ridhee, Rann and Muthu. They're famous for their delicious buffets; couples have plenty of gourmet options for the ceremony and reception.

Tip: If you’re adventurous enough to explore further, wanting to make the best use of the trip, the Mask Museum, a river safari, whale watching, and architect Geoffrey Bawa’s estates at Lunuganga are at hand.

Non-citizen couples are asked to:

  • Arrive 4 working days prior to the wedding with required documents.
  • Confirm wedding dates 2 weeks earlier.

Contact

Phone: 94 91 5555 000
Email : ha.ebiz.lk@aitkenspence.lk

Address

Galle Road, Ahungalla, Sri Lanka

9.  Talalla Beach Resort, Tangalle

Source: tripadvisor.com

Venues and Packages

Here, at the Talalla Beach Resort, there’s hardly anything negative. An Exclusive Bungalow Discount awaits you on offer for groups of between 10-20. This opulently spacious bungalow priced at USD 400 per night for bookings between April 1st to November 30th, comes with 7 double/twin rooms, and 6 dorm beds. There’s a large and cosy dining table or lounge area perfect for family privacy, too.

To shake off the pressure, you can make it a glamorous celebration with the mix of Sri Lanka and European weddings. This is evident in the package offered inclusive of treats such as the Sri Lankan master of ceremonies, traditional poruwa, chantings, saree and national knit for the couple, to name a few.

What about catering? Expect either a sumptuous wedding preparation predominantly Sri Lankan or a 5 course western ala carte menu.

Non-citizen couples are asked to:

  • Arrive 5 working days prior to the wedding with all required documents.

Contact
Phone: 94 41 22 59 171
Email: groups@talallaretreat.com

Address

Sampaya House, Talalla South, Gandara, Matara

8. Jetwing Beach, Negombo

Source: mysrilankaholidays.com

Venues and Packages

It’s not easy avoiding one of the leading hotel chains in Sri Lanka if you want to make your specialised wedding stand out. Both, an ostentatious ballroom for a maximum of 450 to 500 guests, and a neat rustic arrangement within earshot of the shattering waters, here, you can expect offers with plenty of perks within 3 standard wedding packages, rest assured:

  • Gold Wedding Package @ USD 2000.00 per couple
  • Silver Wedding Package @ USD 1750.00 per couple
  • Platinum Wedding Package @ USD 2500.00 per couple

Catering

An appetising provision of gourmet cuisine and bar services can be neatly planned to leave a lasting impression on the jolly guests.

Contact

Phone: 0772 443 087/0774 584 317
Email: reservations@jetwinghotels.com

Address

Porutota Road, Ethukale, Negombo


7. Saman Villas, Bentota

Source: tropicalsky.co.uk

Couples in favour of the exotic and a desire to watch an ochre sky soften to sherbet pink against a majestic shoreline, should make a dash to Saman Villas for your big day.

Venues and Packages

Weddings overlooking the Laccadive Sea, will have you cherish them for eternity. Licensed for civil ceremonies, The Saman Villas events team, will arrange for either a Beach or Garden Ceremony with 16 wedding packages carrying an assortment of benefits inclusive of meeting your catering needs. There is a detailed insight regarding prices, decorations, and the utilizing of equipment here.

Contact

Phone: 94 342 275 435
Email: weddings@samanvilla.com

Address

Aturuwella, Bentota

6. Taprobana Wadduwa, Wadduwa

Source: hotels.com

If you are on the lookout for something palatial and unconventional, Taprobana Wadduwa by Asia Leisure, a 45 minutes drive from Colombo, is one such exemplar of that art.  
 
Venues and Packages

To take home a celebration drenched in the hum and purr of coastal memories, The Glow Ballroom can be orchestrated indoors offering peace and seclusion. The Great Outdoors is accessible on request to evoke a nautical feel for the ones unable to resist the watery beck and call.

Taprobana Wadduwa’s website includes a comprehensive availability of pricing details and services offered by the resort. On a further note, these packages come under four categories which are heavily infused in Sri Lankan traditions.

What about the wedding night? Couples are spoilt for choice.16 Deluxe Allure rooms featuring a mini bar, in-room dining, among others with a spectacular view of the Indian Ocean are available along with other options like Shimmer Suites anticipating your arrival. All you have to do is pick one.

Contact

Phone: 94 38 228 30005
Email: events@taprobana.lk

Address

354/16 Rathnayake Road, Thalpitiya, Wadduwa

5. Vivanta by Taj, Bentota

Source: tripadvisor.co.uk

Venues and Packages

Managed by a dedicated team who ensures your preferences are carefully met in order to make your day unforgettable, the venue facilities allow infinite opportunities. Banquet Lawn, equipped with its open-air styling facing an uninterrupted ocean view can accommodate a capacity of 350 guests. While the Orchid provides space for a maximum of 250 persons to make the most of a glorious evening soiree, Sea View Terrace can hold upto 150 guests. Complete with 160 well-furnished rooms to choose from, overlooking rich gardens, at Vivanta by Taj, the king-sized suites would also make a memorable wedding night.  
 
Catering

In the case of catering, the hotel follows the concept of ‘themed catering experiences.’ This means the scrumptious menus are exquisitely tuned and prepared by expert chefs befitting to your personal taste and choice; assuring that the guests will be inspired by zest, pleasure and vibrancy, all at once.

Contact

Phone: 94 34 555 5555
Email: vivanta.bentota@tajhotels.com

Address

National Holiday Resort Bentota, Galle

4. The Fortress Resort & Spa Sri Lanka, Galle

Source: smallmiracle-srilanka.co.uk

The Fortress Resort & Spa Sri Lanka located at in beautiful Galle is more than just a kiss from sea mermaids (if there are any!) This lovely resort’s architecture exudes of Dutch colonial involvement, and to have your wedding organised here, at the unspoiled lawns, will lend a pleasant aura indeed.

Venues and Packages

Taking a closer look at the resort, the two packages offered sound exceptionally good. To fit your needs, the Moonstone Package at USD 1,700 will include a wedding planner, arch, location and set-up together with champagne, cocktails, manicure and pedicure treatments for the bride, bridal bouquet, wedding albums, among others on offer. The Star Sapphire Package priced at USD 2,965 is bulkier and face-slapping exotic. There’s additionally a 3 course garden dinner for the couple, local dancers, drummers and fire-eaters to zap the spirit, beauty facials, 30 minute ayurvedic consultation and an hour's spa treatment.

Non-citizen couples are asked to:

  • Arrive 5 working days prior to the wedding with all required documents.

Contact

Phone: 94 91 438 9400
Email: reservations@thefortress.lk

Address

Koggala, Habaraduwa, Galle

3. The Kingsbury, Colombo

Source: thekingsburyhotel.com

A revamp has pinched the Kingsbury Hotel to stand higher with poise. Today, this ambitious edifice is well-fed on frills and state-of-the-art excellence. Smack in the heart of Colombo, it is perfect for a flashy nuptial gathering.

Venues and Packages

The awards-garlanded hotel comes with positive Sri Lankan hospitality. Taking in an array of function space complete with high-speed internet, to have your dream wedding here, seems like a marvellous idea.  For example, consider The Balmoral, fit for an occupancy of 500 guests; seated under the shockingly rich chandelier, enjoying a generous slice of the sea. Charming Victorian and The Winchester, on the other hand are adequately equipped to accommodate up to 200 guests. Thinking of celebrating the big day exchanging vows with a twist? The Oval, located at the lobby level for 500 guests is willing to offer you just that. Where exactly? On the hotel’s expansive lawn. There are few other rooms for small wedding parties and receptions as well.

Contents, including limousine car service, sparkling wine, room for dressing purposes, overnight stay for the couple, are all complimentary. Do look forward to a promising list of packages that include appetizers, soups, salads, drinks, rice dishes, desserts offered by the hotel.

  • Bronze @ Rs. 4,900 per person
  • Silver @ Rs. 5,100 per person
  • Gold @ Rs. 5,500 per person
  • Platinum @ Rs. 5,700 per person

Catering

Guaranteed with five-star stature, The Kingsbury, boasts of dedicated experience. So, the gourmet listed on the website sounds eclectically strong, featuring almost the globe on a platter. Among the delectable smörgåsbord of ingredients, if and when you get round to ordering, you will have Mongolian Mexican, Singaporean, Indian, Moroccan, to name a few.

Contact

Phone: 94 112 421 221
Email: info@thekingsburyhotel.com

Address

Janadhipathi Mawatha, Colombo

2. Mt. Lavinia Hotel, Mt. Lavinia

Source: mountlaviniahotel.com

Nestled amidst the untroubled coastal stretch of Mount Lavinia, this classic destination unendingly oozes of the old-world charm. There is a blend of heritage and novelty at play here.

Venues and Packages

The Mt. Lavinia Hotel is well known for embracing diversity, starting from civil, non-denominational and religious ceremonies. The majestic indoor Empire Ballroom accommodates a capacity of 450 guests, exuding an immaculate finish, with a seven-tier sparkling chandelier and the colonial touch still intact. There’s Regency Room gifted not only with a wood-panelled hall, but the pavilion providing a prime view of the ocean, for a seating of 250 guests. If you want to consume less of the inner regality,Tropical Hut, is made accessible to entertain 120 guests inside the hut where they can indulge in an idyllic setting.

Non-citizen couples are asked to:

  • Arrive in Sri Lanka 7 working days prior to the wedding date.
  • Be present in the hotel 4 nights before the wedding with all required documents.

Catering

The hotel catering service allows guests to savour quality buffet styled dishes for an entertaining wedding occasion on request. Been voted, the ‘preferred’ caterer for outdoor events across the island, Mount Lavinia Hotel takes its pride in 30 years of experience.  

Contact

Phone: 94 112 711 711
Email: info@mountlaviniahotel.com

Address

100, Hotel Road, Mount Lavinia

1. Galle Face Hotel, Colombo

Source: gallefacehotel.com

The grand dame is set against a moment’s walk to the sparkly waters and a five hectare urban park in Colombo, easily providing a plethora of photographic opportunities for an exquisite ceremony.
 
Venues and Packages

With aplomb, couples accepting the Galle Face Hotel as their wedding destination, are asked to choose between Grand Ballroom or Jubilee Ballroom. The two supremely comfortable venues for 250 guests, is laden with aristocratic architecture and furniture; not forgetting those gorgeous arched windows invoking a sense of pomp - the extraordinary kind.

The Jubilee Package priced at Rs 6,800 net per person and the Imperial Package costing Rs. 7,800 net per person, features an enviable stock of complimentary extras including a bridal suite on the wedding day, champagne, traditional dancers, ceremonial horse carriage, bag pipers, and cake with a ceremonial sword.

What’s more? With the dawning of 2017, savings of 15% have been declared on weddings to be held until the 31st of March 2017, along with a 10% for bridesmaids weddings and generation weddings scheduled until  December 2017. Turns out, these are golden possibilities prompting not to miss a celebration willing to take dip in a splash of luxury.

Catering

Menus at Galle Face Hotel have it modern and local. The kitchen is supervised by world-class chefs to prepare a wealth of cuisine and fresh cocktails to wash it all down.

To add, this year, you will find Sawan weddings vowing to accommodate up to 1000 guests priced at Rs. 4000 per person, halal endorsed. And for those of you opting for church weddings, complimentary canapes are on offer for a count of 150 guests at the church which is to be collected from the hotel.

Contact

Phone: 94 112 541 010
Email: weddings@gallefacehotel.net

Address

 2, Galle Rd, Colombo 03
