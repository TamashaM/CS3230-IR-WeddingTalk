Top Skincare Products To Get Honeymoon Ready

November 01, 2017 3:51 pm

Just as your body needs substenance to function, your skin too has its own needs. 

The mantra ‘Exfoliate, Cleanse, Hydrate’, says it all. 

The honeymoon is one of those special moments in a woman’s life, where we go for that extra mile to look great. Of course, it starts with your skin. Surprisingly, our local industry has some special treats for you all.

Planning a wedding can be stressful. In par with it comes the pressure to look perfect. Despite all the trouble and struggling to go through it all, it just doesn’t end there. 

Here’s to making things easier; 

The first and most essential part of your skin care routine would be finding an exfoliator. It helps remove dead skin cells and unclog blackheads. Then comes the cleanser. The purpose of a cleanser would be to remove oil and dirt off your skin (added benefit of being able to remove makeup). Finally, comes your toner and moisturiser; the purpose of a toner would be to keep your skin dry and germ free (oily and acne-prone skin types take note) and for those with dry skin I would recommend purchasing a moisturizer. Using a face mask once-twice a week would also help keep your skin radiant. 

Let’s not forget about your body that needs extra attention too. Your body is much simpler than the face. It is often noted that your body is not as sensitive unlike the face. There’s a two step rule when taking care of your body; exfoliate and hydrate. 

Top products recommended to keep that healthy glow;

4ever Skin Natural Coffee Scrub 60g

LKR 550
 

It consists of natural coffee and virgin coconut oil, in a creamy consistency. This can be used for combination skin types. Easily available at any supermarket around Colombo. Recommended to use on face twice a week.

 

Janet Ayurveda Pimples Out Peppermint Face Wash

LKR 150 onwards

 

 

 

Made with natural extracts from Peppermint, Clove, Eucalyptus and Camphor. Just as the name suggests it is for acne prone skin, nevertheless its ability to deep cleanse makes it a perfect everyday use cleanser for any skin type (unless you have extremely dry skin). The amazing minty smell definitely helps in relieving stress too.

 

Natures Secret Deep Cleansing Milk Aloe Vera

LKR 245 onwards

Made with aloe vera and virgin coconut oil. A daily use cleanser suitable for dry skin. It has a milky consistency that helps rejuvenate and moisturise dry skin.

 

Viana Cucumber Toner

LKR 210 onwards

 

It is enriched with cucumber extracts that is soft and gentle on the skin. Cucumber has the benefit of having the same pH level as our skin, which helps it evenly balance. I wouldn’t recommend this if you have dry sky, preferable if you invested in a moisturiser instead.

 

Natures Secret Aloe 94% Gel

LKR 260 onwards

It is made of organic aloe vera, and has several uses for our skin. It is a gel based moisturiser suitable for all skin types. The benefits of Aloe Vera are no news to us Sri Lankans.

 

4ever Skin Naturals Kohomba Kaha Face Pack

LKR 230 onwards

Made of neem, turmeric and virgin coconut oil. It is a deep cleansing, purifying and soothing face pack that helps keeps that natural glow. A minimum use of once a week is sufficient.

 

Viana Apricot Scrub

LKR 395 onwards

This is made of walnut shell grains along with apricot oil, almond oil and plant extracts. It is gentle, yet still manages to clog out impurities.  Recommended to use on body twice a week.

 

Nature Secrets Rose Body Lotion

LKR 195 onwards

Includes rose extracts, organic virgin coconut oil, olive oil, castor oil and tamarind seed extracts. Suitable for all skin types, due to its various hydrating properties that gets through to any skin type.

 

4ever Skin Natural Daily Moisturizer Sun Control SPF30+ 

LKR 400 onwards

It has UVA and UVB protectors to help keep your skin protected from the harmful rays of the sun, through all that wedding shopping and honeymoon weather.

Let’s get on deck to knock out those husbands with flawless silky smooth skin.

Happy wishes to a wonderful honeymoon.
